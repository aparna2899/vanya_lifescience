About Us

Vanya Lifesciences is a Delhi based company engaged in offering a wide variety of high quality products. Since our inception, we constantly strive for a sustainable growth. The company's operation is well managed by three visionaries and with their support, we have been able to execute all the business operations in an efficient manner.


Our Vision, Mission & Values

Vision


Our vision is to assure consistent quality and timely delivery at competitive price by adopting process supported by proven technologies, which are cost effective and safe which will help us meeting the changing requirements of the customers.

Mission


Our mission is to maintain growth, in terms of value through the eyes of our patients. We indulge in continuous innovation and development of new health products and to make them available at affordable prices. In all our efforts towards the same, we follow our primary values of excellence, quality, and teamwork.

Value


Vanya Lifesciences focuses on traditional values coupled with innovative management. We believe in optimizing opportunities to the fullest. In this manner, we identify the unmet medical needs and capitalize on them to optimum levels ahead of competition.
